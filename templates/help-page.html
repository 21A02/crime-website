Know your website!

This website is an attempt to visulaze the Crime data in a simple and easy manner. Whenever you will visit this website you can easily go through the crime statistics and understand the data that is fetched from various news websites.
The analysis of crime rates for Indian states or union territories is very useful in studying if the crime rate is increasing or decreasing in a particular region. If the crime has increased, necessary measures can be taken by the officials to study why the crime has increased and also how to reduce the crime rate in that region.

Know your data sources!


We have collected the crime data from various resources.

  • The historic data used for training the model has been taken from Kaggle.
  • We have used web scraping to fetch crime data from news websites.
